Two heroes fight each other.
Each of them attacks the other, at turns, until someone's health is depleted.
Hero atacking first is chosen at random.
Heroes have different stats, which makes the winner, unpredictable.

GameManager - holding all the fight logic as well and some helper functions and properties.
TurnManager - manages turns

Heroes statistics:

Superman
    health - 200
    attack - 20
    critical chance - 20% chance to make critical strike ( doubles the attack damage )
    deflect 5% ( returns dmg to the attacker )

Batman
    health - 150
    attack - 5
    critical chance - 40% chance to make critical strike ( doubles the attack damage )
    deflect - 35% ( returns dmg to the attacker )
